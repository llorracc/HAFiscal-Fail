The bonus check is announced to frictionless households one period in advance.

In the period that the bonus arrives, households consume 15.5% of the bonus amount.
One period after the bonus arrives, households consume 11.6% of the bonus amount.
Two periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 9.0% of the bonus amount.
Three periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 7.0% of the bonus amount.
Four periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 5.6% of the bonus amount.
